If you are unsure of your version press ALT+U, go to the 'Shared' tab and in the bottom right 
(the bread macro) you can see your version.

If you didn't update to V2.3 and you're currently on V2.2 and you want to manually update to v2.3 AND you are
using the old hotbar orders then use the macros directly below
Make sure you have copied the culinarian layout first and then press ALT+U to bring up the macro editor
and make macros with the lines below

/hotbar copy CUL 2 ALC 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 ALC 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 ALC 4
/hotbar copy CUL 2 WVR 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 WVR 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 WVR 4
/hotbar copy CUL 2 LTW 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 LTW 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 LTW 4
/hotbar copy CUL 2 GSM 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 GSM 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 GSM 4
/hotbar copy CUL 2 ARM 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 ARM 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 ARM 4

/hotbar copy CUL 2 BSM 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 BSM 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 BSM 4
/hotbar copy CUL 2 CRP 2
/hotbar copy CUL 3 CRP 3
/hotbar copy CUL 4 CRP 4
